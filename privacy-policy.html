Privacy Policy

Effective July 2, 2026. Developed by Bansi Savaj. This policy explains how Curious Chaos handles account data, facts, answers, reactions, purchases, notifications, analytics, crash reports, and ads.

Firebase Apple Sign In Google Sign-In AdMob StoreKit

Information We Collect

  • Account details from Apple or Google sign-in, such as name, email, and account identifier.
  • User content, including facts, answers, reactions, favorites, shares, and other activity you submit.
  • Usage, device, crash, and performance data.
  • Purchase and subscription status, including fact passes and renewals.
  • Notification preference and app settings.

How We Use Information

  • run, improve, and personalize the app
  • save your progress, streaks, and content
  • process subscriptions and fact passes
  • show or hide ads based on subscription status
  • monitor crashes, bugs, and performance
  • send notifications you enable

Third-Party Services

Curious Chaos may use Firebase for authentication, Firestore storage, analytics, and crash reporting; Google Sign-In and Sign in with Apple for account access; AdMob for ads; and Apple StoreKit for purchases and subscriptions. These services may process data under their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

  • sign out of the app
  • disable notifications in iOS settings
  • manage ad preferences in iOS settings
  • request account data deletion by contacting support
